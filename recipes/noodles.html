Desciption

Instant noodles are a quick and easy meal option, made by precooking and drying noodles, which can be rehydrated with hot water. They are often flavored with seasoning packets and are popular worldwide for their convenience and affordability.

They come in various flavors and styles, catering to different tastes and preferences. Some people like to enhance their instant noodles by adding vegetables, eggs, or meat to create a more nutritious and satisfying meal.

Ingedients

  • Instant noodles
  • Seasoning packet
  • Hot water

Steps

  1. Empty seasoning packet onto noodles
  2. Pour hot water up to fill line
  3. Let noodles cook for 3 minutes
  4. Mix with chopsticks
